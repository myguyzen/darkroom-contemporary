01

What the Institute is

"Curiosity is not a theme. It is a method. Questions are not a prelude to the work — they are the work."

A Darkroom Contemporary platform

The Institute of Curiosity sits alongside Darkroom Contemporary as its open, public-facing platform. Two brands, one engine. Credibility flows from Darkroom into the Institute — and the Institute sustains the studio that makes the work possible.

The Institute of Curiosity is Darkroom Contemporary's platform for movement practice, emerging artists, public discourse and screen dance. It is the living, open side of what Darkroom builds in private — the studio door left ajar for the curious.

The closest reference is William Kentridge's Centre for the Less Good Idea: an artist company and a public platform sitting side by side, credibility flowing one way, ideas flowing everywhere. What Darkroom makes in the studio, the Institute opens up to others.

The Institute operates from Darkroom's permanent studio on Bree Street, Cape Town. It is where the company's methods become available — through movement classes the public can attend, conversations the industry can join, labs where emerging artists work alongside the company, and free studio sessions for the freelance dance community.

None of the programmes here deliver a finished answer. They all make space to not know yet.

South Africa is the only country on the African continent actively integrating new technologies into contemporary dance at this scale. The Institute exists to ensure the ecosystem grows, not just the company.

Studio Practice — book a class

Movement classes for adults. Practise alongside working artists.

Studio Practice is the Institute's ticketed public class programme — morning and evening sessions taught by Darkroom's resident artists at the Bree Street studio. Classes are technical and exploratory, suitable for adults with some movement experience.

Classes run weekly. Drop-in welcome. Class packs save you money. Professional and student rates are available: upload a portfolio or student card to receive your discount code at checkout.
